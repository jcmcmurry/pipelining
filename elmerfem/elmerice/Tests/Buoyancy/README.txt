Test the Buoyancy solver
An iceberg of 100m thickness floating on sea (sea level = 0) 
with an accumulation of 0.5 on its upper surface
and an equivalent melting of 0.5 on the bottom surface
Obviously, the iceberg elevation and thickness should not change.

How to run the test:
---------------------
ElmerGrid 1 2 mesh.grd
ElmerSolver buoyancy.sif
